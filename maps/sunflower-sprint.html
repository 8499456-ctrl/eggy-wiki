Sunflower Sprint

Race Moderate Best character: Fox Egg

📝 Overview

Sunflower Sprint is a sunny, cheerful race track winding through golden sunflower fields. Despite its friendly appearance, this map has a few tricky sections that separate casual players from pros. The key to mastering this map lies in the windmill shortcut — if you time it right, you can skip a huge portion of the track.

⚠️ Hazards: Swinging hay bales (70% mark), rotating windmill blades (40% mark), collapsing wooden fences

🏁 Race Guide

First 30%: Straight sections with mild obstacles. Use this to build momentum and position yourself in the top 3. 30-60%: The windmill section. Jump onto the rotating blades and ride them to the upper path. This saves ~3 seconds. 60-80%: Swinging hay bales. Time your dash — don't rush, wait for the gap. Final 20%: Open sprint to the finish. Use your dash boost here.

🗺️ Shortcuts

⚡ Windmill Blade Ride

Jump on the windmill blades when they're horizontal — ride to the upper platform. If you miss, you'll fall back to the main path and lose 2 seconds.

👍 Pro Tips

  • Fox Egg's dash cooldown is invaluable here — use it to chain the windmill shortcut with the final sprint
  • Bunny Egg's high jump can skip the hay bale section entirely by jumping over the fence
  • Penguin Egg has no advantage here — no ice surfaces on this map
  • The windmill shortcut is easiest to hit from the right side of the track
  • If you miss the shortcut, don't panic — the main path is only 2 seconds slower
