Frozen Peak

Race Expert Best character: Penguin Egg

📝 Overview

Frozen Peak is an ice-covered race track that demands precision and control. The slippery surfaces make every movement risky, but mastering the ice physics gives you a massive advantage. Penguin Egg's 15% ice speed boost makes it the undisputed best character here.

⚠️ Hazards: Ice patches (reduced traction), falling icicles, hidden ice crevasses, slippery bridges

🏁 Race Guide

First 30%: Gentle ice slopes. Build momentum — smooth curves, not sharp turns. 30-50%: Hidden ice cave shortcut. Look for the crack in the wall at the 50% mark. 50-80%: Zigzag ice paths. Penguins rule here — use the ice slide. Final 20%: The igloo boost. Jump through the igloo for a speed boost.

🗺️ Shortcuts

⚡ Ice Cave Passage

At the 50% mark, look for a wall crack on the right side. Jump through it to enter a hidden ice cave that shaves 4 seconds off your time.

👍 Pro Tips

  • Penguin Egg is mandatory for competitive Frozen Peak — the 15% ice boost is too powerful to pass up
  • Never use dash on ice unless you're about to fall — you'll slide uncontrollably
  • Use smooth, wide turns instead of sharp corners
  • The igloo at 80% doesn't just look cute — jumping through it gives a 2-second speed boost
  • Practice the ice cave shortcut in casual mode before ranked

⚡ ❄️ Hidden Ice Ramp

There's a nearly invisible speed boost ramp on the far right edge of the main ice slide. It's flush with the ice texture, making it hard to spot. Finding and using this ramp consistently is what separates average Frozen Peak players from top-tier ones. How to execute: 1. When entering the long ice slide section, slide to the far right edge 2. Look for a slightly lighter-colored strip on the ice surface 3. Keep your egg centered on that strip — you'll feel a speed boost 4. The ramp launches you at double speed past 2-3 obstacles 5. Use Penguin Egg for this map — ice doesn't slow you down at all

⏱️ Phase-by-Phase Guide

🟢 Early Game (Phase 1)

The track is wide and open. Focus on building momentum on the ice sections. Penguin Egg has a massive advantage here — other characters lose speed on ice while Penguin Egg maintains full traction. Follow the center path for the safest route. - Position: center-right, aiming for the hidden ramp - Aggression level: Low — focus on getting ahead, not fighting - Key resource: Save dash for the mid-map obstacle cluster

🟡 Mid Game (Phase 2)

The track narrows and introduces icicle traps and collapsing ice platforms. The icicles fall with a 3-second audio cue — count to 3 after the warning sound, then dash through. Collapsing platforms are marked by cracking ice textures. - Position: follow the left edge after the icicle section - Aggression level: Medium — knocking opponents into the ice water is an easy elimination - Key resource: Jump pads are available — use them sparingly

🔴 Late Game (Phase 3)

The final stretch is a gauntlet of rotating ice blocks and wind gusts. Wait for the rotating blocks to line up before jumping — rushing here costs you the race. The wind gusts push you backward if you don't time your dashes. - Position: behind the leader, waiting for the right moment - Aggression level: High — this is the final straight - Key resource: Save double jump for the last gap before the finish line

🕐 Hazard Timing

Ice slide length: ~15 seconds at full speed Icicle fall warning: 3 seconds of audio + visual cue Collapsing platform cycle: New platform every 8 seconds Final gauntlet: ~20 seconds with perfect execution

🥚 Best Characters for This Map

🐧 Penguin Egg — Overwhelming #1 pick. Ice physics don't affect you. 🥚 Classic Egg — Decent if you understand ice mechanics 🐰 Bunny Egg — The high jump helps on rotating ice blocks 🦊 Fox Egg — Fast dash helps recover from ice slides
