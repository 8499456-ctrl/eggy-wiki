Underwater Escape

Survival Hard Best character: Bunny Egg

📝 Overview

Underwater Escape is a vertical survival map where water levels rise continuously. Players must climb through multiple elevation levels while avoiding hazards and other players. The last egg on the highest platform wins.

⚠️ Hazards: Rising water (instant elimination), jellyfish shock zones, collapsing coral platforms

🛡️ Survival Guide

Early game: Stay one level above the water — don't camp at the waterline. Mid game: Move up with the water level. Use bubble indicators to find the next platform. Late game: Center platforms are safest. The edges are dangerous.

👍 Pro Tips

  • Bunny Egg's high jump is the best ability for this map — it reaches upper platforms in one jump
  • Fish eggs create bubbles where the next platform will spawn — watch for them
  • Don't rush to the highest level early — you'll be exposed to attacks from below
  • Stay near edges in mid-game, move to center in late game
  • The water rises in predictable intervals — use the pause between rises to recover your position

⚡ 🌊 Hidden Underwater Tunnel

After the air bubble refill section, there's a hidden tunnel on the lower left wall that bypasses the entire jellyfish gauntlet. This is the riskiest but most rewarding shortcut in the game. How to execute: 1. After passing the air bubble cluster at the 40-second mark, look down 2. There's a dark opening in the lower left wall of the underwater section 3. Dive down and swim into the opening — it's easy to miss 4. The tunnel curves right then up — follow the glow 5. You emerge right before the finish line, bypassing the jellyfish completely 6. ⚠️ Critical: Make sure you refilled at the last air bubble cluster before attempting this. The tunnel takes 8 seconds to swim through and there's no air refill inside.

⏱️ Phase-by-Phase Guide

🟢 Early Game (Phase 1)

The start is a shallow pool with currents pushing you in different directions. Swim against the current on the left side — it's slightly slower initially but sets you up perfectly for the air bubble section. - Position: left side, swimming against the current - Aggression level: Low — the currents make combat difficult anyway - Key resource: Watch the current timer — it changes direction every 12 seconds

🟡 Mid Game (Phase 2)

The air bubble section determines your race. You need to hit 3-4 air bubbles to have enough oxygen for the rest of the map. Don't rush through — a dead egg can't finish. The bubbles respawn on a 20-second cycle. - Position: follow the bubble chain, but don't panic-click - Aggression level: Medium — players low on oxygen are easy targets - Key resource: This is where you decide: hidden tunnel or main path?

🔴 Late Game (Phase 3)

If you took the tunnel: You emerge at the final stretch with a 10-second lead. Just don't mess up the last jump. If you took the main path: The jellyfish gauntlet requires precise dodging. Jellyfish pulse every 3 seconds — dodge between pulses. Don't try to push opponents here, focus on survival. - Tunnel path: Final jump + finish line. Easy. - Main path: Dodge jellyfish pulses, wait for openings - Key resource: If low on oxygen after the tunnel, you made a mistake

🕐 Hazard Timing

Current direction change: Every 12 seconds Air bubble respawn: 20 seconds Hidden tunnel: 8 seconds swim time Jellyfish pulse: Every 3 seconds
