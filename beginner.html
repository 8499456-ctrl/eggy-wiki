🎮 Beginner's Guide

New to Eggy Party? Here's everything you need to know to get started!

1Download & Install

Eggy Party is available on iOS, Android, and PC. Search "Eggy Party" in your app store or visit the official website to download. It's free to play!

2Game Modes

There are three main game modes:

  • Race Mode — 8 players race to the finish line. Obstacles and shortcuts everywhere!
  • Survival Mode — Last egg standing wins. Platforms disappear, hazards appear.
  • Team Mode — 4v4 matches with objectives like capture the flag.

3Basic Controls

Swipe to move, tap to jump. Double-tap for a double jump. Hold and swipe to dash. Master these basics before trying advanced techniques!

4Tips for Beginners

  • Complete daily missions for free rewards
  • Save gems for limited-time outfits
  • Practice race maps in casual mode first
  • Watch the minimap — it shows hazards before they appear
  • Join a club to earn team bonuses

5First Week Goals

Day 1-3: Complete tutorial, play 10 races. Day 4-5: Try survival mode, unlock your first outfit. Day 6-7: Join a club, play ranked mode. By week 2, you'll be competing with the best!
