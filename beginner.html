1Download & Installation

Eggy Party is a free-to-play mobile and PC game developed by NetEase. It's available on all major platforms:

  • iOS — Search "Eggy Party" on the App Store. ~1.2 GB download.
  • Android — Available on Google Play and the official NetEase website. ~900 MB.
  • PC — Download from the official Eggy Party website. ~2 GB.
💡 Tip: Download over Wi-Fi — the initial download is large but subsequent updates are much smaller.

The game supports cross-platform play, so you can play with friends regardless of device.

2Game Modes Explained

Eggy Party has three permanent game modes plus a rotating event mode:

🏁 Race Mode (8 Players)

The most popular mode. 8 players race through obstacle-filled tracks to reach the finish line. First three to finish get ranked rewards. Key mechanics include jumping over gaps, dodging obstacles, and using shortcuts.

🛡️ Survival Mode (8 Players)

Last egg standing wins. Platforms shrink, disappear, or become hazardous over time. Players can knock each other off platforms. Each match lasts 2-4 minutes.

👥 Team Mode (4v4)

Two teams of four compete in objective-based matches. Modes include Capture the Flag, King of the Hill, and Egg Collection. Communication is key — use the in-game quick chat!

🎪 Event Mode (Limited Time)

Rotating special modes that change every 1-2 weeks. Past events include Egg Frenzy (16-player free-for-all), Zombie Mode, and Gravity Shift.

3Controls & Mechanics

Master these controls to gain an edge over other players:

  • Move — Swipe/drag to move your egg in any direction
  • Jump — Tap to jump. Jump again mid-air for a double jump
  • Dash — Swipe quickly in a direction to dash (has cooldown)
  • Grab — Tap the grab button near another player to push or hold them
  • Item Use — Tap item button when you have a power-up

Advanced Techniques

  • Short Hop — Quick tap to barely leave the ground. Useful for platform edges.
  • Dash + Jump — Dash off a platform edge then jump for extended distance.
  • Edge Grab — If you fall off a platform, you can grab the edge and pull yourself back up if you're close enough.

4Choosing Your Character

Your character choice matters! Each egg has unique stats and abilities:

  • 🥚 Classic Egg — All-rounder. Great for learning the game. Balanced speed and jump.
  • 🐰 Bunny Egg — Higher jump. Ideal for race maps with lots of vertical sections.
  • 🦊 Fox Egg — Faster dash cooldown. Best for advanced players who use dash frequently.
  • 🐱 Cat Egg — Soft landing + faster recovery. #1 recommended for Survival mode.
  • 🐼 Panda Egg — Harder to knock over. Good for Team mode as a defender.
  • 🐧 Penguin Egg — Faster on ice. Situational but dominates ice maps.

Visit our full characters page for detailed stats, unlock conditions, and tier rankings.

5Currencies & Items

Eggy Party has several currencies. Here's what you need to know:

  • 💎 Gems — Premium currency. Earned through gameplay, events, and codes. Used for outfits, crates, and battle pass.
  • 🪙 Coins — Free currency. Earned from matches and daily missions. Used for basic items.
  • 🎫 Tickets — Required to enter ranked matches. You get 5 free per day + more from missions.
  • 📦 Crates — Random rewards. Open for a chance at outfits, gems, or coins.
💡 Tip: Save your gems for limited-time outfits. Don't spend them on crates — the drop rates are low. Use our codes page for free gems!

6Tips for Beginners

🏁 Race Mode Tips

  • Learn the shortcuts on each map — they can shave 3-5 seconds off your time
  • Don't always take the shortest path — sometimes the safer route is faster if you avoid mistakes
  • Use double jump conservatively — save it for crossing wide gaps
  • Watch the starting countdown — a good start puts you ahead of half the players immediately

🛡️ Survival Mode Tips

  • Stay on the edges during the first 30 seconds — the center is a war zone
  • Let other players fight — avoid confrontation until the late game
  • Always have an escape route planned
  • Save shield power-ups for the final moments

👥 Team Mode Tips

  • Stick with your team — solo players get picked off easily
  • Use quick chat to communicate (tap the speech bubble icon)
  • Designate roles: 1-2 attackers, 1 defender, 1 flex

7First Week Goals

Follow this roadmap to go from complete beginner to competitive player in one week:

  • Day 1-2: Complete all tutorial missions. Play 10 casual races. Unlock your first outfit.
  • Day 3-4: Try Survival mode. Practice advanced techniques (dash+jump, edge grab). Join a club.
  • Day 5-6: Play ranked mode. Learn 2-3 race map shortcuts. Complete daily missions for rewards.
  • Day 7: Aim for top 5 in ranked consistently. Start saving for a premium outfit. Join the community forum!
✨ Remember: Eggy Party is meant to be fun! Don't stress about winning — focus on improving one skill at a time. See you on the leaderboard!

8🏆 Ranked Mode Guide

Ranked mode is where the real competition begins. Here's everything you need to climb the ladder:

Tier System

  • Egg V → Egg I (Bronze equivalent) — Every player starts here. Learn the basics in a competitive setting.
  • Silver V → Silver I — Players understand basic mechanics. Focus on consistency.
  • Gold V → Gold I — Advanced techniques expected. Short hops, dash+jump combos, and map knowledge required.
  • Platinum V → Platinum I — Top 15% of players. Competitive matches with skilled opponents.
  • Diamond V → Diamond I — Top 3% of players. Near-perfect execution expected.
  • Legend — Top 100 players on the server. The elite.

Climbing Strategies

  • Warm up before ranked: Play 2-3 casual matches to get in the zone before jumping into ranked.
  • Focus on consistency, not flashy plays: Finishing 3rd every game is better than 1st one game and 8th the next.
  • Know when to take breaks: After 2 consecutive losses, take a 5-minute break. Tilted play leads to more losses.
  • Master 3 maps at a time: Don't try to learn all maps at once. Focus on 3, learn every shortcut, then move on.
  • Watch replays of top players: After a loss, watch the replay from the winner's perspective.

Ranked Rewards

  • Season End Rewards: Exclusive outfits, gems, and title based on highest tier achieved.
  • Weekly Milestones: Bonus gems for reaching certain point thresholds each week.
  • Placement Matches: First 10 ranked matches determine your starting tier. Try your absolute hardest!
⚡ Pro tip: Use a game accelerator for ranked matches. High ping causes delayed inputs that can cost you a win. Check our free accelerator page.

9🎭 Outfits & Customization

Express yourself with hundreds of outfits, accessories, and effects. Here's how the system works:

Outfit Rarity Tiers

  • Common (⚪) — Basic color variants. Earned from leveling up or cheap coin purchases.
  • Rare (🔵) — Themed costumes. Available in the shop for gems or from crate drops.
  • Epic (🟣) — Detailed costumes with special effects. Often tied to events or battle pass.
  • Legendary (🟠) — Premium costumes with unique animations, effects, and sounds. Limited availability.

Battle Pass System

  • Free Track: Earn basic rewards — coins, common crates, a few gems.
  • Premium Track ($4.99/month): Unlock exclusive epic outfits, premium crates, and bonus gems.
  • Deluxe Track ($9.99/month): Everything in Premium + instant tier skips, a legendary outfit, and exclusive nameplate.
  • The battle pass lasts one season (approx. 6-8 weeks). Levels carry over between seasons.

Limited & Collab Outfits

  • Eggy Party regularly collaborates with popular franchises (Sanrio, SpongeBob, and more).
  • Collab outfits are available for a limited time only — usually 2 weeks.
  • If you miss a collab outfit, it may never return. Grab it while it's available!
  • Limited outfits often cost 960-1,440 gems each.
💡 Tip: Save your gems for limited-time collaboration outfits. Regular premium outfits rotate back eventually, but collabs may not.

10🤝 Team Strategies

Team mode is all about coordination. Here's how to dominate as a group:

Role Assignments

  • Attacker (1-2 players): Fast characters (Fox Egg, Minty). Focus on objective and pressuring opponents.
  • Defender (1 player): Tanky characters (Panda Egg, Rocky). Protect your base and flag carrier.
  • Flex (1 player): Balanced character (Classic Egg, Yeggy). Adapt to whatever the team needs.

Communication Tips

  • Use the quick chat wheel — "Help!", "Retreat!", "Follow me!", "Watch out!"
  • Pre-set your quick chat phrases before the match starts.
  • In Capture the Flag: Always call out "Enemy has the flag!" with the quick chat.
  • In King of the Hill: "Gather at the hill!" is the most important call.

Team Compositions

  • Speed Comp: 2x Fox Egg + 1x Minty + 1x Cat Egg — Hyper-aggressive, fast objective play.
  • Defense Comp: 2x Panda Egg + 1x Rocky + 1x Yeggy — Slow but nearly unkillable.
  • Balanced Comp: 1 of each role with flexible characters — Safe but effective.
  • Support Comp: Unicorn Egg (speed boost aura) + 3 fast attackers — Devastating in coordinated teams.
🤝 Pro tip: Play with the same team regularly. Chemistry and unspoken coordination is worth more than individual skill.

11🗺️ Map-Specific Tips

Each map has unique shortcuts and strategies. Here are tips for the most popular maps:

Candy Arena

  • Key shortcut: On the candy cane section, bounce off the top of the red stripe for extra height — this skips an entire platform section.
  • Best character: Bunny Egg (high jump makes the vertical sections much easier).
  • Watch out for: The collapsing gumdrop platforms near the end — time your jump carefully.

Frozen Peak

  • Key shortcut: On the ice slide, stay to the far right edge — there's a hidden speed boost ramp.
  • Best character: Penguin Egg (slides on ice without losing speed — this is a massive advantage).
  • Watch out for: The icicle trap section. Count to 3 after the warning sound, then dash through.

Sunflower Sprint

  • Key shortcut: On the rotating sunflower section, jump from the highest petal directly to the second-to-last platform.
  • Best character: Fox Egg (flat terrain rewards speed over jump height).
  • Watch out for: The wind gust traps between sections — they push you back if you don't time your dash.

Underwater Escape

  • Key shortcut: After the air bubble section, drop down immediately and swim left — there's a hidden tunnel.
  • Best character: Bulu (faster on water surfaces).
  • Watch out for: The rising tide mechanic at the 45-second mark — stay on high ground.
💡 Tip: Practice each map's shortcut 10 times in casual mode before trying it in ranked. Consistency beats hero plays.

12💎 Event Guide

Limited-time events are the best way to earn free rewards. Here's how to maximize every event:

Event Types

  • Festival Events — Seasonal celebrations (Lunar New Year, Halloween, Summer Festival). Usually 2-3 weeks long with exclusive free outfits.
  • Collab Events — Brand partnerships (SpongeBob, Kuromi, etc.). Limited-time themed content and outfits.
  • Competitive Events — Tournament-style events with leaderboard rankings. Top players earn exclusive titles and rewards.
  • Login Events — Just log in daily for free rewards. Never miss these — they take 30 seconds.

Maximizing Event Rewards

  • Check events daily: Open the event tab every day when you log in. Many events have daily missions that reset.
  • Prioritize limited exclusive items: If an event offers an exclusive outfit, make that your #1 goal before the event ends.
  • Use event currency efficiently: Don't spend event currency on consumables — save for the exclusive items.
  • Stack missions: If multiple events are active, check if their missions overlap (e.g., "Play 5 races" counts for both).
  • Last-day rush: Events often have a 2x points bonus on the final day. Save some missions for this boost.

Event Calendar (2026)

  • January-March: Lunar New Year Festival — Exclusive zodiac outfit, red envelope rewards.
  • April-June: Spring Celebration — Cherry blossom themed outfits and housing items.
  • July-September: Summer Splash — Beach outfits, water-themed maps, swimsuit collection.
  • October-December: Halloween Fest + Winter Wonderland — Spooky costumes, snow maps, holiday outfits.
  • Events rotate every 1-2 weeks between major festivals. New collaborations are announced monthly.
⚡ Pro tip: Join the official Eggy Party Discord or follow their social media for event announcements before they go live. You can plan your gem spending in advance!

13📱 Settings Optimization

Proper settings can give you a significant competitive advantage. Here's the optimal setup:

Graphics Settings (Mobile)

  • Frame Rate: Set to "High" (60fps). On weaker devices, "Medium" (30fps) is acceptable but puts you at a disadvantage.
  • Resolution: "High" if your device can handle it. Low resolution makes distant obstacles harder to see.
  • Effects Quality: "Low" — reduces visual clutter from other players' abilities and makes the path clearer.
  • Shadow Quality: "Off" — shadows consume GPU resources and provide no gameplay benefit.

Control Settings (Mobile)

  • Button Layout: Customize your layout. Put dash button where your thumb naturally rests.
  • Sensitivity: Start at 100% and adjust up if you find movement sluggish. Pro players use 120-150%.
  • Auto-Aim: Turn it OFF for competitive play. Auto-aim can pull your camera in the wrong direction.
  • Vibration: Off — saves battery and removes distracting feedback during intense moments.

Network Optimization

  • WiFi vs Mobile Data: 5G/4G is often more stable than public WiFi for gaming.
  • Ping Check: Play with ping under 80ms for competitive modes. Above 120ms = noticeable lag.
  • Background Apps: Close all background apps before playing. Streaming music also adds latency.
  • Game Accelerator: For players in Asia connecting to international servers, a game accelerator is essential. Check our recommended list.

PC Settings

  • Window Mode: Fullscreen > Borderless > Windowed. Fullscreen gives the best performance.
  • VSync: OFF — adds input lag. Use your GPU's Fast Sync option if you experience screen tearing.
  • Anti-Aliasing: Low or Off — reduces blur and keeps the image sharp for spotting obstacles.
  • Textures: Medium is enough. High textures don't help gameplay but consume VRAM.
💡 Tip: After changing graphics settings, play 2-3 casual matches to ensure everything feels smooth. Never change settings right before a ranked match!
