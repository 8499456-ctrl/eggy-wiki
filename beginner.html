1Download & Installation

Eggy Party is a free-to-play mobile and PC game developed by NetEase. It's available on all major platforms:

  • iOS — Search "Eggy Party" on the App Store. ~1.2 GB download.
  • Android — Available on Google Play and the official NetEase website. ~900 MB.
  • PC — Download from the official Eggy Party website. ~2 GB.
💡 Tip: Download over Wi-Fi — the initial download is large but subsequent updates are much smaller.

The game supports cross-platform play, so you can play with friends regardless of device.

2Game Modes Explained

Eggy Party has three permanent game modes plus a rotating event mode:

🏁 Race Mode (8 Players)

The most popular mode. 8 players race through obstacle-filled tracks to reach the finish line. First three to finish get ranked rewards. Key mechanics include jumping over gaps, dodging obstacles, and using shortcuts.

🛡️ Survival Mode (8 Players)

Last egg standing wins. Platforms shrink, disappear, or become hazardous over time. Players can knock each other off platforms. Each match lasts 2-4 minutes.

👥 Team Mode (4v4)

Two teams of four compete in objective-based matches. Modes include Capture the Flag, King of the Hill, and Egg Collection. Communication is key — use the in-game quick chat!

🎪 Event Mode (Limited Time)

Rotating special modes that change every 1-2 weeks. Past events include Egg Frenzy (16-player free-for-all), Zombie Mode, and Gravity Shift.

3Controls & Mechanics

Master these controls to gain an edge over other players:

  • Move — Swipe/drag to move your egg in any direction
  • Jump — Tap to jump. Jump again mid-air for a double jump
  • Dash — Swipe quickly in a direction to dash (has cooldown)
  • Grab — Tap the grab button near another player to push or hold them
  • Item Use — Tap item button when you have a power-up

Advanced Techniques

  • Short Hop — Quick tap to barely leave the ground. Useful for platform edges.
  • Dash + Jump — Dash off a platform edge then jump for extended distance.
  • Edge Grab — If you fall off a platform, you can grab the edge and pull yourself back up if you're close enough.

4Choosing Your Character

Your character choice matters! Each egg has unique stats and abilities:

  • 🥚 Classic Egg — All-rounder. Great for learning the game. Balanced speed and jump.
  • 🐰 Bunny Egg — Higher jump. Ideal for race maps with lots of vertical sections.
  • 🦊 Fox Egg — Faster dash cooldown. Best for advanced players who use dash frequently.
  • 🐱 Cat Egg — Soft landing + faster recovery. #1 recommended for Survival mode.
  • 🐼 Panda Egg — Harder to knock over. Good for Team mode as a defender.
  • 🐧 Penguin Egg — Faster on ice. Situational but dominates ice maps.

Visit our full characters page for detailed stats, unlock conditions, and tier rankings.

5Currencies & Items

Eggy Party has several currencies. Here's what you need to know:

  • 💎 Gems — Premium currency. Earned through gameplay, events, and codes. Used for outfits, crates, and battle pass.
  • 🪙 Coins — Free currency. Earned from matches and daily missions. Used for basic items.
  • 🎫 Tickets — Required to enter ranked matches. You get 5 free per day + more from missions.
  • 📦 Crates — Random rewards. Open for a chance at outfits, gems, or coins.
💡 Tip: Save your gems for limited-time outfits. Don't spend them on crates — the drop rates are low. Use our codes page for free gems!

6Tips for Beginners

🏁 Race Mode Tips

  • Learn the shortcuts on each map — they can shave 3-5 seconds off your time
  • Don't always take the shortest path — sometimes the safer route is faster if you avoid mistakes
  • Use double jump conservatively — save it for crossing wide gaps
  • Watch the starting countdown — a good start puts you ahead of half the players immediately

🛡️ Survival Mode Tips

  • Stay on the edges during the first 30 seconds — the center is a war zone
  • Let other players fight — avoid confrontation until the late game
  • Always have an escape route planned
  • Save shield power-ups for the final moments

👥 Team Mode Tips

  • Stick with your team — solo players get picked off easily
  • Use quick chat to communicate (tap the speech bubble icon)
  • Designate roles: 1-2 attackers, 1 defender, 1 flex

7First Week Goals

Follow this roadmap to go from complete beginner to competitive player in one week:

  • Day 1-2: Complete all tutorial missions. Play 10 casual races. Unlock your first outfit.
  • Day 3-4: Try Survival mode. Practice advanced techniques (dash+jump, edge grab). Join a club.
  • Day 5-6: Play ranked mode. Learn 2-3 race map shortcuts. Complete daily missions for rewards.
  • Day 7: Aim for top 5 in ranked consistently. Start saving for a premium outfit. Join the community forum!
✨ Remember: Eggy Party is meant to be fun! Don't stress about winning — focus on improving one skill at a time. See you on the leaderboard!
