🗺️ Map Guides

Complete map strategies — race shortcuts, survival tactics, and team mode tips for every stage.

🏁
Race

Sunflower Sprint

A sunny race track through golden fields. Moderate difficulty with one major shortcut.
💡 Tips:
  • The windmill shortcut at the 40% mark saves ~3 seconds — jump onto the rotating blades and ride them to the upper path
  • Penguin Egg's ice slide doesn't work here (no ice surfaces)
  • Watch out for the swinging hay bales at the 70% mark — time your dash through
  • Best character: Fox Egg (dash to chain the shortcuts faster)
🌋
Race

Volcano Rush

Race down the volcano slope. Fast-paced with hazardous lava pools.
💡 Tips:
  • The lava geyser at the 25% mark can launch you onto a secret upper path — stand on it for 2 seconds
  • Don't jump over lava pools too early — wait until the last moment for maximum speed
  • Bunny Egg is surprisingly good here — the extra jump height helps skip a segment at the 60% mark
  • Avoid the inner lane on the final straight — the collapsing bridge forces you to detour
🎪
Survival

Candy Arena

Rotating candy-themed platform. Hazards include candy cannons and disappearing tiles.
💡 Tips:
  • The platform rotates clockwise — always move opposite to the rotation to stay centered
  • Candy cannons fire every 10 seconds in a predictable pattern — watch for the telltale sparkle
  • Disappearing tiles follow a set order: memorize it to plan your path
  • Cat Egg is the MVP here — soft landing saves you when tiles disappear beneath you
❄️
Race Best for

Frozen Peak

Ice-covered race track with extremely slippery surfaces. High skill ceiling.
💡 Tips:
  • Penguin Egg is the undisputed king here — 15% ice speed boost is massive
  • Don't use dash on ice unless necessary — you'll slide uncontrollably
  • The hidden ice cave at the 50% mark is faster than the main path but requires precise jumping
  • Momentum is everything — keep moving in smooth curves, not sharp turns
  • Look for the igloo at the 80% mark — jumping through it activates a speed boost
🏰
Team

Castle Clash

4v4 capture the flag. Asymmetric map with two castle bases connected by a bridge.
💡 Tips:
  • Panda Egg is the best defender — hard to knock over when carrying the flag
  • The middle bridge is a death trap — use the underground tunnel instead
  • Assign one player as dedicated flag carrier with max speed build
  • The side cannons can launch you across the map for a quick escape — learn the timing
🌊
Survival

Underwater Escape

Rising water levels force players upward. Vertical survival map with multiple elevation levels.
💡 Tips:
  • Always stay one platform level above the water — don't camp at the current waterline
  • Bubbles rising from the depths indicate where the next platform will appear
  • Don't jump to the highest level too early — you'll be exposed to attacks from all sides
  • Stay near the edges during mid-game, move to center in late game
  • Bunny Egg's high jump is useful here for reaching upper platforms quickly
