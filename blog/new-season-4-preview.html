Season 4 Preview: What's Coming to Eggy Party

July 6, 2026 · 3 min read

Season 4 of Eggy Party is just around the corner, and leaks from the test server are already pouring in. Here's everything we know so far about the biggest update yet.

📅 Expected release: Late July 2026. The official announcement is expected within the next two weeks.

🗺️ New Maps

Two brand new race maps and one survival map have been spotted in the test build:

  • Sky Garden — A vertical race map through floating islands. Wind currents can boost or throw you off course.
  • Neon Express — A cyberpunk-themed subway track. Trains and moving platforms are the main obstacles.
  • Mushroom Marsh — Survival map with shrinking platforms and poisonous spore clouds.

👗 New Outfits

Season 4's battle pass will include 8 new outfits. The most anticipated ones:

  • Cyber Egg — Full neon armor with animated LED effects. Season Pass level 50 reward.
  • Mermaid Egg — Flowing tail animation when moving. Ranked reward for top 500.
  • Ninja Egg — Stealth-themed outfit with smoke trail effect. Shop item, 1,800 gems.

🎮 New Game Mode: Egg Frenzy

A new 16-player limited-time mode has been found in the game files. Rules are simple: collect eggs scattered across the map while avoiding other players. The player with the most eggs when time runs out wins. Power-ups include speed boosts, shields, and egg magnets.

⚖️ Balance Changes

Based on community feedback, several balance changes are expected:

  • Fox Egg dash cooldown increased from 8s → 10s
  • Bunny Egg jump height reduced by 10%
  • Penguin Egg ice slide speed increased by 15%
  • Classic Egg gets a slight speed buff to stay competitive

💎 Quality of Life

Season 4 is also bringing some long-requested features:

  • Practice mode for race maps (no other players)
  • Replay system — watch your last 10 matches
  • Friend list improvements — see online status and current mode
  • Cross-region party support (finally!)

We'll update this post as more details emerge from official channels. Stay tuned!
