XP Math: How Much Do You Need to Play?

To reach Level 100, you need 20,000 total XP (200 XP per level):

  • Daily only: 500 XP/day × 45 days = 22,500 XP ✅ (enough with 3-4 days buffer)
  • Daily + Weekly: 500 XP/day + 3,200 XP/week = 22,500 + 20,800 = 43,300 XP ✅ (way more than enough)
  • Casual player: Just doing dailies (300 XP) × 45 = 13,500 XP ❌ (not enough)

Bottom line: If you play ~30 minutes daily and complete most weeklies, you'll hit 100 with weeks to spare. If you only play every other day, you'll need to grind on weekends.

🎯 Pro tip for XP efficiency: Focus on modes that complete multiple weekly missions at once. Survival Mode (top 3 finishes) overlaps with "Win 5 matches," "Complete Survival Mode," and "Play 3 matches" missions. One solid Survival session can check 3-4 boxes in 20 minutes.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Battle Pass reset if I buy it mid-season?

No. If you buy the Premium pass at Level 60, you immediately unlock all rewards from Levels 1-60 on the Premium track. Progress carries over from your free track level.

Can I complete the pass without paying?

Yes. The free track gives you 30 prisms, 3 outfit fragments (60% toward a free outfit), and profile cosmetics. You miss the exclusive premium outfit, pet skin, and bonus prisms.

Do leftover Battle Pass levels turn into anything?

After Level 100, every 2,000 XP gives you a "Overflow Chest" with 5 Egg Coins and a small chance at a prism. Not amazing, but better than nothing.

What's the fastest game mode for XP?

Race mode is the fastest XP-per-minute because matches are shortest (3-4 minutes). Survival Mode gives more weekly mission XP but each match takes 8-12 minutes.
