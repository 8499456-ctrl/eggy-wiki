🎮 Season 6 New Characters Review

New abilities, stats, and tier ranking for S6 characters

Home › Blog › S6 Characters Review
CharactersSeason 6Review

Season 6 introduces 3 new characters to Eggy Party, each with unique abilities that shake up the meta. Here's our full review and ranking for each one.

🆕 New Characters Overview

🌟 Nova

Ability: Star Burst — Release a burst of energy that stuns nearby opponents for 1.5 seconds

Unlock: Season 6 Battle Pass Tier 30

Rating: S-Tier for Survival, A-Tier for Race

Nova's Star Burst is one of the best crowd control abilities in the game. In survival mode, using it when surrounded by 3+ opponents can turn the tide completely. The 1.5 second stun is enough time to escape a dangerous situation or knock someone off a platform.

Best for: Survival mode, team mode, and chaotic sections of race maps.

Cooldown: 14 seconds. Can be reduced to 11 seconds with ability upgrade.

🌀 Zephyr

Ability: Wind Dash — Dash forward 2x distance while becoming invincible for 0.8 seconds

Unlock: 800 Eggy Gems from the shop

Rating: S-Tier for Race, B-Tier for Survival

Zephyr's Wind Dash is the best mobility skill in Season 6. The invincibility frames let you pass through hazards and opponent abilities. On race maps with shortcuts, Zephyr can skip entire sections. The double dash distance is especially powerful on Sky Garden's rotating platforms.

Best for: Race mode, any map with high-risk shortcuts.

Cooldown: 16 seconds. Invincibility lasts 0.8 seconds (enough to pass most single obstacles).

🛡️ Shard

Ability: Crystal Wall — Create a wall that blocks projectiles and slows opponents who touch it

Unlock: Special Event — "Crystal Cavern" (limited time)

Rating: A-Tier for Team and Survival

Shard's Crystal Wall is a strategic ability that rewards smart placement. In team mode, place the wall at chokepoints to split the enemy team. In survival, use it to block incoming projectiles. The wall lasts 4 seconds and has 3 charges before going on cooldown.

Best for: Team mode, survival mode, narrow corridors.

Cooldown: 20 seconds (after using all 3 charges). Each charge regenerates every 7 seconds.

📊 Updated Tier List (With S6 Characters)

  • S-Tier: Zephyr (Race), Nova (Survival), Minty (Race), Rosie (Survival)
  • A-Tier: Shard (Team), Rocky (Team), Yeggy (All-rounder)
  • B-Tier: Bulu (Survival), Minty (Survival)
💡 Which to unlock first? If you play race mode, unlock Zephyr first — the Wind Dash is game-changing for race maps. If you prefer survival or team modes, unlock Nova for the best crowd control ability.

🎯 Upgrade Priority

Once you unlock a new character, focus upgrades in this order:

  1. Ability cooldown reduction — Lower cooldown = more plays per match
  2. Ability duration/effect — Longer or stronger ability effects
  3. Base stats — Speed and defense are universal, ability upgrades are character-specific
🔥 Pro tip: You can preview all character abilities in the Training mode before unlocking. Spend 10 minutes testing each new character to see which fits your playstyle before committing your gems or battle pass tiers.

🏆 Verdict

Season 6 brings the most balanced character roster yet. There's no single "must-have" character for all modes — each new addition excels in specific scenarios. Zephyr is the most exciting addition for race enthusiasts, while Nova and Shard add meaningful depth to team and survival modes.
