Best Characters Tier List

July 10, 2026 • 6 min read

Choosing the right character in Eggy Party can make or break your run. With so many eggs to pick from, we've put together a definitive tier list based on stats, ability usefulness, and performance across all game modes.

Tiers are ranked S (best) through C (below average). Keep in mind that even C-tier characters can win in the right hands — this list is about overall consistency.

S-Tier — Top Picks

These characters dominate in almost every mode. If you have one unlocked, use them.

CharacterWhy S-TierBest Mode
BuluBest all-rounder with a strong speed boost ability that charges fast. Excellent for both racing and survival.Race
MintyIce shield ability gives you a free pass through traps. Unmatched on ice maps like Frozen Peak.Survival
RosieHealing aura that helps teammates and yourself. A must-pick for team modes.Team

A-Tier — Strong Choices

Reliable and powerful, but with a minor weakness or longer cooldown holding them back.

CharacterWhy A-TierBest Mode
RockyGreat defensive stats and a rock-skin ability that absorbs hits. A bit slow for pure racing maps.Survival
YeggyHigh starting speed and a dash ability. Falls off slightly in longer races due to stamina drain.Race
Classic EggBalanced stats with no major weakness. The jack-of-all-trades, master of none.All

B-Tier — Situational

These characters shine in specific modes or maps but struggle in others.

CharacterWhy B-TierBest Mode
Cat EggNimble jumps help on platform-heavy maps, but ability is less useful in open racing.Survival
Fox EggSpeed burst is decent, but the cooldown is longer than most. Good for short races.Race
Panda EggDefensive buff for teammates — valuable in teams, weak in solo.Team

C-Tier — Needs Buffs

These characters are fun but outclassed. Use them if you like the design, but don't expect top placements.

CharacterWhy C-TierBest Mode
Penguin EggSlippery movement and weak abilities. Fun on ice maps but outclassed by Minty.Survival
Dragon EggFire burst looks cool but has a tiny hitbox and high cooldown. High skill floor.Race
Unicorn EggRainbow trail doesn't provide a real gameplay advantage. Mostly cosmetic.All

Final Tips

Your tier list pick matters most in Ranked Mode. In casual play, use whoever you enjoy. Remember that character abilities can be upgraded — an S-tier character at level 1 may still lose to a well-played B-tier at max level.

We'll update this tier list each season. Bookmark this page and check back after the next patch!
