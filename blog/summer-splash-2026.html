🏖️ Summer Splash 2026 — Full Event Guide

📅 July 20, 2026 · 👤 Wiki · ⏱ 5 min read

Eggy Party's biggest seasonal event is here! Summer Splash 2026 brings water-themed maps, exclusive beach outfits, and a ton of free rewards. Running from July 18 to August 10, here's everything you need to know to max out your rewards.

⏰ Event Period: July 18 – August 10, 2026
🎯 Daily Reset: 05:00 UTC

🌊 New Limited-Time Maps

🏖️ Coconut Beach Race

A brand-new race map set on a tropical island. Dodge flying coconuts, surf across tidal waves, and bounce on inflatable platforms. Shortcuts require precise timing — and a bit of luck.

  • Difficulty: ⭐⭐ (Medium)
  • Best for: All characters with high acceleration
  • Hidden path: At the 40% mark, take the left inflatable ramp for a shortcut that skips the tidal wave section

🤿 Deep Dive Survival

A floating platform battle map. As water levels rise, the safe zone shrinks. Push opponents into the water — last egg standing wins.

  • Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
  • Tip: Save your knockback skills for when the platform shrinks. One well-placed push = instant elimination

🍉 Melon Mayhem (Team)

4v4 team mode. Collect watermelons scattered around the map and deliver them to your team's juicer. The team with the most juice after 3 minutes wins.

  • Best strategy: Assign 2 attackers, 1 defender, 1 collector
  • Power-up spawns: Speed boosts on the left lane, shields near the center

👕 Free & Premium Outfits

🏆 Free reward alert: Complete 15 daily missions to unlock the "Sunset Surfer" outfit for Yeggy — no gacha required!

  • 🏆 Sunset Surfer (Yeggy) — FREE — Complete 15 daily event missions
  • 💰 Mermaid Dream (Rosie) — Premium Pass Lv.50 — Exclusive to Summer Pass holders
  • 💰 Lifeguard Bulu — Shop (960 Gems) — Limited time, won't return until next summer
  • 🏆 Watermelon Slice (Back item) — FREE — Log in 7 days during event
  • 💰 Tropical Floatie (Mount) — Gacha — Inflatable flamingo mount, 0.5% drop rate

🎯 Event Mission Guide

📅 Daily Missions (Resets every day)

  • Play 1 match on Coconut Beach Race → 50 Shells 🐚
  • Complete 3 survival rounds → 50 Shells
  • Knock out 5 opponents total → 30 Shells
  • Log in (free daily) → 20 Shells

🏁 Challenge Missions (One-time)

  • Win Coconut Beach Race 3 times → 200 Shells + "Beach Legend" title
  • Survive 5 min total in Deep Dive → 150 Shells
  • Collect 50 watermelons in Melon Mayhem → 180 Shells
  • Share event screenshot on social media → 100 Shells

🎁 Reward Track (Shells)

  • 200 Shells → 50 Gems
  • 500 Shells → Summer Profile Frame
  • 1,000 Shells → Sunset Surfer outfit (Yeggy)
  • 1,500 Shells → 100 Gems
  • 2,000 Shells → Exclusive "Summer Champion" Emote

💡 Pro tip: Focus on daily missions first. You need 15 daily completions for the free outfit — that's roughly 12-15 minutes per day. The challenge missions can be done casually over the event period.

🌴 New Summer Pass (Season Pass)

The Summer Splash pass runs alongside the event at $9.99 USD:

  • Free track: 300 Gems, 5 Mystery Boxes, 1 Emote
  • Premium track: Mermaid Dream Rosie outfit, 800 Gems, 10 Mystery Boxes, 2 Exclusive Emotes, Summer Splash Banner
  • Bonus: Premium pass holders get +20% Shells from all activities

💎 Free Gems Guide

Wondering how many free Gems you can earn? Here's the breakdown:

  • Daily missions (23 days × 100 Gems worth of Shells) = ~2,300 Gems equivalent
  • Challenge missions = 450 Gems + 200 Shells
  • Login rewards (7 days) = 150 Gems
  • Total free value: ~3,000 Gems + exclusive outfit

💬 Final Verdict

Summer Splash 2026 is one of Eggy Party's best seasonal events yet. Three fun new maps, a generous free track, and the Yeggy beach skin being free is a nice touch. If you play casually, you'll easily unlock the free outfit and most rewards. The Premium Pass is worth it if you want the Rosie Mermaid Dream skin and the extra Shell boost.

See you on the beach, eggs! 🏖️🥚
