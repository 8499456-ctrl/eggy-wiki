🎮 New Season 5 Preview — Everything Coming to Eggy Party

📅 July 12, 2026 · ✍️ Wiki · 📖 5 min read
← Back to Blog
newsseason 5update

Eggy Party Season 4 is winding down, and leaks and teasers for Season 5 are starting to surface. Here's everything we know so far — release date, new maps, new outfits, and the rumored new game mode.

📌 Expected Release

Based on the current season calendar, Season 5 is expected to launch around early August 2026. The exact date hasn't been confirmed yet, but the in-game countdown on the Season Pass screen says 22 days remaining as of July 12.

🗺️ New Maps (Leaked)

Sky Garden (Rumored Name)

A vertical map set among floating islands. Players will need to navigate rising platforms, wind tunnels, and collapsing cloud bridges. Early testers describe it as "the most vertical map yet" — expect lots of falling.

Neon Boulevard

A cyberpunk-themed city track with moving billboards, laser barriers, and a massive loop-de-loop at the center. This looks like it'll be the fastest map in the game, with lots of boost pads placed close together.

👗 New Outfits

  • Galactic Explorer Set — Space-themed outfit with a helmet visor that lights up. Rarity: Epic.
  • Pirate Captain — Swashbuckler style with an eyepatch and tiny parrot companion. Rarity: Rare.
  • Neon Rider — Cyberpunk bodysuit with glowing trim. Rarity: Epic.
  • Watermelon Slice — A goofy full-body fruit costume. Rarity: Common (likely free from season pass).

🎯 New Game Mode: Co-op Challenge

The biggest rumored addition is a 2-player co-op mode where you and a teammate must complete obstacle courses together. Both players need to reach the end within a time limit — if one falls, you both restart from the last checkpoint.

This would be the first team-based PvE mode in Eggy Party and could change how the game is played. No official confirmation yet, but dataminers found references to "coop_stage_01" through "coop_stage_05" in the latest update files.

🏆 Season Pass Changes

Based on feedback from Season 4, the dev team is reportedly making these changes:

  • ✅ More free-track rewards (shards + coins per level instead of every 5 levels)
  • ✅ Lower level cap for premium pass (from 80 → 65 levels, same rewards)
  • ✅ Daily mission XP increased by 20%
  • ✅ Weekly challenges now include a "play with friends" option

💡 What to Do Before Season 5

  • Spend your Season 4 currency — it won't carry over to Season 5
  • Complete your current Battle Pass — don't leave those last few rewards behind
  • Practice on existing maps — the new maps will be tougher, having the fundamentals down helps
  • Save your coins — new outfits will be available day one, you'll want to have a stockpile ready
