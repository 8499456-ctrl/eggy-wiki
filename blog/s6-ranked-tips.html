🏆 Season 6 Ranked Mode Tips

Climb the leaderboard with these pro strategies

Home › Blog › S6 Ranked Tips
RankedSeason 6Guide

Season 6 of Eggy Party brings a new ranked season with updated maps, new character balance changes, and tougher competition. Here's everything you need to climb from Gold to Eggcellent tier.

🥇 Best Characters for Ranked in S6

While all characters are viable in casual play, ranked requires specific picks depending on the game mode:

S-Tier for Ranked

  • Minty — Speed boost ability is invaluable for race modes. Her signature skill gives a 30% speed increase for 3 seconds with a 12-second cooldown.
  • Rosie — Best for survival modes. Her shield blocks one hit completely, which can save you from elimination at critical moments.
  • Rocky — Top tier for team modes. His rock throw disrupts opponents and can knock them off platforms at just the right moment.

A-Tier for Ranked

  • Yeggy — Versatile with a decent skill for both race and survival. Good for beginners in ranked.
  • Bulu — Strong in survival mode due to the area denial bubble ability. Less useful in race modes.
💡 Pro tip: In ranked, having one main character for race modes (Minty) and one for survival/team modes (Rosie) is better than trying to master one character for everything.

🗺️ Map-Specific Strategies

Sky Garden (New S6 Map)

  • The floating platforms rotate clockwise — learn the timing to skip ahead of the pack
  • Save your skill for the final section with moving pendulums
  • Take the high path at the fork (it's slightly longer but avoids crowd chaos)

Neon Boulevard (S6 Map)

  • The neon strips boost your speed — stay on them as much as possible
  • Watch out for the color-changing barriers (red = danger, green = safe)
  • Cut corners tightly on the final U-turn to gain 2-3 positions

Frozen Peak

  • On ice slides, don't jump — sliding without jumping maintains momentum
  • Use the snowball piles strategically: throw behind you to slow pursuers
  • The shortcut on the right at 60% mark saves 2 seconds if executed perfectly

🎯 Positioning Tips for Ranked

  • Start strong: In race modes, get to top 3 within the first 10 seconds. It's much harder to overtake from the back
  • Don't panic-use skills: Wait for a bottleneck or a high-risk section where your skill will give maximum advantage
  • Watch the minimap: Know where opponents are. If 3 are behind you, save your defensive skill
  • Play conservatively in top 8: Once you're in the top 8, don't take unnecessary risks. Consistency beats flashy plays

📈 Understanding Ranked Scoring

Season 6 ranked scoring works as follows:

  • Race Mode: 1st = +25 pts, 2nd = +18 pts, 3rd = +12 pts, 4th-8th = +5 pts, bottom 4 = 0 to -10 pts
  • Survival Mode: Last egg standing = +30 pts, top 50% = +10 pts, eliminated early = -15 pts
  • Team Mode: Win = +20 pts, close loss = +5 pts, blowout loss = -10 pts
🔥 Key insight: It's better to consistently place 4th-6th (+5 pts) than to go all-in and risk 12th place (-10 pts). Consistency is how you climb in Season 6.

⚡ Advanced Techniques

  • Dive roll cancel: After a dive roll, tap jump immediately to cancel the recovery animation. This saves 0.3 seconds per roll.
  • Edge slide: Near ledges, slide and jump at the very edge to gain extra distance. Practice this on Frozen Peak's ice bridges.
  • Skill weaving: Use your skill right before a jump pad to get extra height and distance from the pad.
