📦 Items & Props

Complete list of Eggy Party power-ups, consumables, and special items

⚡ Power-ups

Collect these during races for a temporary advantage.

💨

Speed Boost

Power-up

Temporarily increases movement speed by 40% for 5 seconds. Great for overtaking on straightaways.

🛡️

Shield

Power-up

Blocks one incoming obstacle or attack. Lasts until hit or 10 seconds pass.

🍃

Jump Boost

Power-up

Increases jump height by 60% for 8 seconds. Essential for shortcut routes.

🧲

Magnet

Power-up

Attracts all nearby coins and items within a 10m radius for 6 seconds.

🌀

Tornado

Power-up

Launches a tornado that slows down opponents ahead. Lasts 4 seconds on hit.

💎

Extra Life

Consumable

Revives you one time after falling off the map. Automatically consumed on death.

🎁 Consumables

Items you can use in the lobby or between matches.

📦

Egg Crate (Common)

Loot Box

Common crate containing random common-rarity outfit pieces or coins.

✨

Egg Crate (Rare)

Loot Box

Rare crate with guaranteed rare or better outfit item.

👑

Egg Crate (Legendary)

Loot Box

Guaranteed legendary outfit item. Highest rarity crate.

🪙

Coin Bag

Currency

Contains 500-2000 in-game coins. Used to purchase items from the shop.

💎

Gem Pack

Premium Currency

Premium currency pack. 100/300/600/1200 gems available in the shop.

🎟️

Event Ticket

Limited

Entry ticket for limited-time events. Earned via daily missions or purchased.
