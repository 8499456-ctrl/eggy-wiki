❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Eggy Party

🎮 What is Eggy Party?
Eggy Party (蛋仔派对) is a multiplayer party game developed by NetEase where players control cute egg characters and compete across various race, survival, and team game modes. It's available on iOS, Android, and PC.
📱 Is Eggy Party free to play?
Yes! Eggy Party is free to play on all platforms. In-app purchases are available for cosmetic items like outfits and accessories, but they do not affect gameplay.
🎴 What are active Eggy Party codes?
We maintain an up-to-date list of active codes on our Codes page. Codes are released regularly through events, social media, and partnerships. Check back weekly!
🐣 How do I unlock new characters?
Characters can be unlocked by collecting eggs through gameplay, completing missions, or purchasing them with in-game coins. Some limited characters are only available during special events. Visit our Characters page for details on each egg.
🗺️ How do I find map shortcuts?
Many maps have hidden shortcuts that can give you a significant advantage. Check our Maps page for detailed guides on each map, including shortcut locations and route strategies.
👗 How do I get free outfits?
Free outfits can be earned through daily missions, event participation, leveling up your character, and redeeming special codes. Check our Free Outfits Guide for a complete list.
🏆 Which character is the best?
It depends on the game mode! Our Tier List ranks characters by their performance across different modes. Generally, Bulu, Minty, and Rosie are strong picks for most situations.
🔄 How often is Eggy Wiki updated?
We update our content regularly — new guides are published weekly, codes are updated as soon as new ones are released, and tier lists are refreshed each season. Follow our Blog for the latest updates.
📞 How can I contact you?
Visit our Contact page to get in touch. We welcome feedback, suggestions, and corrections!
🔒 Is Eggy Wiki official?
No, Eggy Wiki is a fan-made project and is not affiliated with NetEase. We're created by and for the Eggy Party community.
