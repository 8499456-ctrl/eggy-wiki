📝 Overview

Panda Egg is the ultimate tank. With 60% knockback resistance and a slow-but-steady playstyle, Panda Egg is built to absorb hits that would send other characters flying. The trade-off is the lowest speed and jump in the game, plus the longest dash cooldown.

🎯 How to Unlock

Limited character — was available during Season 1: Panda Paradise event. May return in future events or shop rotations.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Panda Egg is the undisputed king of Survival mode. Let the chaos happen around you.
  • In Team mode, Panda Egg is the best flag defender — nothing moves you.
  • Never pick Panda Egg on race maps. You will lose. Just don't.
