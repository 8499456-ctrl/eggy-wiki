📝 Overview

Penguin Egg has a unique passive: sliding on ice surfaces doesn't reduce speed. While other characters slip and slide on icy terrain, Penguin Egg maintains full control and speed. This makes Penguin Egg the absolute best pick for any map with ice sections.

🎯 How to Unlock

Reach Level 10 or purchase for 1,500 gems from the Character Shop.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Penguin Egg is S-tier on Frozen Peak — the ice sections become your fastest route.
  • Use Penguin Egg's ice control to take risky shortcuts that other characters can't handle.
  • On non-ice maps, Penguin Egg performs as an average character — switch accordingly.
