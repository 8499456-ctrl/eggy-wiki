📝 Overview

Cat Egg is widely considered the best survival mode character. Its passive ability reduces knockback recovery time by 40% — meaning you're back on your feet faster after being hit. Combined with soft landing (no stun from falling), Cat Egg thrives in chaotic situations where other characters would be eliminated.

🎮 Best Game Modes

  • Race Mode — Good — soft landing is useful on tricky maps
  • Survival Mode — Excellent — the #1 survival pick in the game
  • Team Mode — Good — hard to knock off objectives

👍 Tips

  • In survival mode, position yourself near ledges — you can recover from falls others can't
  • Cat Egg's defense stat makes it forgiving for beginners learning survival mode
  • Don't be afraid to get aggressive — you can survive hits that would eliminate other characters
