📝 Overview

Fox Egg is built for speed and recovery. With the highest base speed among special characters and 30% reduced stun duration, Fox Egg can recover from knockbacks faster than any other egg. The trade-off is lower jump height, making Fox Egg a ground-speed specialist.

🎯 How to Unlock

Reach Level 12 or purchase for 1,800 gems from the Character Shop.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Fox Egg's reduced stun is a lifesaver in Survival mode — recover and reposition while opponents are still stunned.
  • On flat maps like Sunflower Sprint, Fox Egg's speed advantage is unmatched.
  • Avoid maps with high platforms where Fox Egg's low jump becomes a major disadvantage.
