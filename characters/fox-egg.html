📝 Overview

Fox Egg's specialty is speed. With the shortest dash cooldown in the game (6 seconds instead of the standard 10), you can chain dashes together for incredible mobility. This is a high-skill, high-reward character — if you can master dash timing, you'll leave opponents in the dust.

🎮 Best Game Modes

  • Race Mode — Excellent — dash cooldown lets you chain shortcuts efficiently
  • Survival Mode — Good — quick dashes help escape danger zones
  • Team Mode — Excellent — best flag carrier due to unmatched mobility

👍 Tips

  • Learn the dash+jump combo — dash off a platform edge then jump for extended distance
  • Save your dash for moments when it matters — don't waste it on small movements
  • Fox Egg is the #1 choice for speedrunners and competitive race mode players
