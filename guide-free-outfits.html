3 Redeem Codes

NetEase regularly releases redeem codes for free outfits, gems, and other items. Codes are usually valid for 24-72 hours, so grab them fast.

Where to Find Codes

  • Official social media — Follow Eggy Party on Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok
  • Live streams — Dev streams often give out exclusive codes
  • Our codes page — We update daily: eggywikigames.com/codes
  • Community Discord — Fans share codes in real-time

How to Redeem

  • In-game: Settings → Redeem Code → Enter code → Claim
  • Website: Go to the official NetEase code redemption page
  • Codes are case-sensitive. Copy and paste to avoid typos.
💡 Tip: Some codes are limited to the first 10,000 redemptions. Check our codes page within an hour of a new code dropping!

5 Level-Up Rewards

As you level up your account, you unlock various free outfits and accessories. These are guaranteed — no RNG involved.

Key Level Milestones

  • Level 5: Basic Color Pack (5 color variants)
  • Level 10: Player Icon Frame
  • Level 20: Casual Weekend Outfit
  • Level 35: Nameplate Decor Pack
  • Level 50: Veteran Egg Costume
  • Level 75: Elite Badge Set
  • Level 100: Legendary Egg Costume
⚡ Pro tip: XP boosts are sometimes available from event rewards or codes. Save them for when you have time to play extended sessions.

7 Social & Community Rewards

Connect with the community and earn free outfits along the way.

Social Outfits

  • Share on social media: Share your victory screen for a free emote pack
  • Refer a friend: Recruit 3 friends who reach level 10 → exclusive Friend Crew outfit
  • Join official Discord: Special Discord role unlocks an in-game nameplate
  • Submit fan art: Selected fan art winners get exclusive Creator outfits
  • Content creator program: Stream Eggy Party to earn exclusive Creator tier outfits

8 Daily Free Crate

Every day, you can open one free crate from the shop. While the drop rate for full outfits is low (~2%), it's completely free and takes 30 seconds.

Free Crate Strategy

  • Open your free crate every single day — don't miss a day
  • Even if you don't get an outfit, you get coins and consumables
  • Save up free crate tickets from events for increased odds during rate-up events
  • During anniversary events, free crates have doubled outfit drop rates
⚡ Pro tip: Set a daily alarm to open your free crate. Missing a single day is a missed opportunity, and 365 crates a year almost guarantee at least a few free outfits.
