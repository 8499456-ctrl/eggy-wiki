📖 About Eggy Wiki

Your community-driven guide to Eggy Party

What is Eggy Wiki?

Eggy Wiki is a fan-made, community-driven resource for Eggy Party (also known as 蛋仔派对), the popular multiplayer party game developed by NetEase. Our mission is to provide accurate, up-to-date information about characters, maps, outfits, game mechanics, and strategies — all in one place, completely free.

Whether you're a brand new player wondering how to start, or a seasoned veteran looking for the latest tier list and codes, Eggy Wiki has you covered.

What We Offer

  • Character Guides — Detailed breakdowns of every egg character's abilities, strengths, and best game modes.
  • Map Strategies — Tips, shortcuts, and route guides for race and survival maps.
  • Outfit Collections — Complete catalog of all outfits with unlock requirements and rarity info.
  • Latest Codes — Updated regularly with active redemption codes for coins and rewards.
  • Community Forum — A place for players to connect, share tips, and discuss the game.
  • Tier Lists — Data-driven rankings to help you pick the best characters.
